Shares Rise Despite Political Fears..
The Jakarta Stock Exchange Index rose 3.745 to 580.074 on volume of 158.8 million shares.
Gainers outnumbered decliners 53 to 37, with 44 shares was unchanged.
Local investors bought second-tier issues, though the political rumors kept investors' mood bearish.
Property company Duta Anggada rose 150 rupiah to 2,300.
